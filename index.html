About Me

Hi my name is Kaden Haggard. I have been a member of DECA for 4 years and am currently serving as the VP of Leadership for Oak Grove High School DECA. As VP of District 4, it is my goal to prepare chapters to feel empowered to gain the most of what DECA has to offer. I also want to show all members the importance of taking control of your future. During my time in DECA, I have advanced to compete at ICDC, as well as placed first in the JEFCOED Navigator’s Cup marketing series. Outside of DECA, I enjoy working closely with my school’s marketing team, performing with the OGHS Marching band, and serving younger students through our peer helper program.

Mission Statement

It is my mission to pass-on a positive example of self-belief. I will ensure my interactions with others reflect upon me and align with who I strive to be. I will commit my career to showing others the importance of service to and the impact you will leave behind.
